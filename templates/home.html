Locite

Simplified Gene Finder

Welcome to Locite! Easily find and visualize
genes with our powerful search tool. Locite
allows you to search for a specific gene, or
find a gene linked to a specific genetic disease
or physical trait.

How to use:
If looking for a specific gene, type the name of
the gene (ex: HBB). If looking for a gene linked
to a genetic disease or physical trait, type in the
genetic disease (ex: Huntington) or physical trait
(ex: Eye Color). The search tool is case sensitive,
make no spelling mistakes.
